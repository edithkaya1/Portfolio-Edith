About Me


I studied business informatics and computer science. During my studies, I began to acquire practical knowledge in the IT department of a large Austrian bank. After graduating, I moved to an international book group where I worked as a software developer, consultant and requirements engineer. I implemented customer projects for our customers in Canada, France, Belgium, Hungary, the Czech Republic, Switzerland and Austria. I worked mainly in the mainframe area and have very good knowledge of COBOL and SQL. In the last few years I started to learn about new technologies and familiarized myself with frontend and backend development. In 2024, I completed 2 courses in C#, a basic web development course and am currently training to become a fullstack WEB developer at CodeFactory in Vienna.
